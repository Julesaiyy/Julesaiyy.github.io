Biography

Here is Julesaiyy. I am a fourth-year undergraduate student at the School of Computing and Artificial Intelligence, Southwest Jiaotong University, advised by Hongjun Wang. My research interests include machine learning (ML), computer vision (CV), data mining (DM), and natural language processing (NLP). Specifically, few-shot/zero-shot learning, recommender systems, and medical image processing are included.
